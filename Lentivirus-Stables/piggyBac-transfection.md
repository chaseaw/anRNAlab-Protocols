Stable PiggyBac Transfection
================================================================================
Description: Transfection of piggyBac plasmids for stable, inducible expression of a gene.

[Go To Protocol](#protocol)

Materials:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  * Complete culture media appropriate for the cells  
    _Complete means appropriate serum and general antibiotics (Pen-Strep)_
  * Serum- and antibiotic-free culture media
  * [FuGENE HD Transfection Reagent](https://www.promega.com/products/luciferase-assays/transfection-reagents/fugene-hd-transfection-reagent/)
  * Cargo plasmid(s) containing gene(s) of interest flanked by ITRs
  * pB-rtTA plasmid (reverse tetracycline transactivator flanked by ITRs)
  * PiggyBac transposase plasmid
  * Selection antibiotics

Equipment Required:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  * Tissue culture hood
  * Cell culture incubator
  * 6-well tissue culture plates
  * Micropipettes

<br/><br/><br/><br/><br/><br/><br/><br/><br/>

___
Protocol:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
### Day 1
**1.** Plate cells in complete media.  
1 desired cell line per well of a 6-well plate 1 day before transfection.  
_Note: the amount of plated cells is cell line-dependent._

### Day 2
**2.** Remove the existing media and add 1.5 mL complete culture media to each well.

**3.** Prepare one transfection tube for each cargo combination. Add the following:

  | Component | Concentration | Quantity | 
  | ---------: | ---------: | :---------- |
  | Serum-free media || **492 - (X<sub>1</sub>+X<sub>2</sub>+X<sub>3</sub>)** |
  | Cargo construct(s)* | 0.5 µg DNA | **X<sub>1</sub>** |
  | pB-rtTA | 0.5 µg DNA | **X<sub>2</sub>** |
  | piggyBac transposase | 1.0 µg DNA | **X<sub>3</sub>** |
  || **Total** | **492** µL |
  
  _Note: Calculate the volume of each plasmid from its measured concentration._  
  _If multiple cargos are used, they should add up to the µg total._

**4.** Add 8 µL FuGENE HD directly into the media/DNA mixture.  
Do not pipette the reagent onto the sides of the tube.  
_Note: this is a 2:1 ratio, but can be optimized per cell line._

**5.** Flick the tube gently to mix.

**6.** Incubate for 10 minutes at room temperature.

**7.** Add each transfection mixture in its entirety dropwise to respective wells.

**8.** Incubate cells for approximately 24 hours.

<br/>

### Day 3

**9.** Replace the media with complete media + selection antibiotics.  
_Note: Selection antibiotic concentrations are cell-type specific._

### Day X

**10.** Culture cells on selection media beyond critical point determined by kill curve.  
Expand surviving cells and validate dox-inducible expression of gene(s) of interest.
