pTETRIS PiggyBac Transfection
================================================================================
Description: Transfection of pTETRIS, rtTA, and PiggyBac transposase plasmids for stable, inducible expression of a gene.

[Go To Protocol](#protocol)

Materials:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  * Complete culture media appropriate for the cells
    ◦ RPMI complete was used in the original protocol
    ◦ 10% FBS and 1% Penicillin/Streptomycin
  * Serum-free DMEM
    ◦ No FBS or Penicillin/Streptomycin
  * [FuGENE HD Transfection Reagent](https://www.promega.com/products/luciferase-assays/transfection-reagents/fugene-hd-transfection-reagent/)
  * pTETRIS plasmid containing the gene of interest
  * pB-rtTA plasmid
  * pCMVβ-PiggyBac transposase plasmid
  * 1.5 mL microcentrifuge tubes
  * Selection media appropriate for the integrated constructs

Equipment Required:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  * Tissue culture hood
  * Cell culture incubator
  * 6-well tissue culture plates
  * Micropipettes

<br/><br/><br/><br/><br/><br/><br/><br/><br/>

___
Protocol:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
**1.** Plate cells one day before transfection.

**2.** Remove the existing media and add **1.5 mL complete culture media** to each well of a 6-well plate.

**3.** Prepare one transfection tube for each pTETRIS construct. Add the following:

  | Component | Amount |
  | ---------: | :---------- |
  | pTETRIS construct | **0.5 µg DNA** |
  | pB-rtTA | **0.5 µg DNA** |
  | pCMVβ-PiggyBac transposase | **1.0 µg DNA** |
  | FuGENE HD | **8 µL** |
  | Serum-free DMEM | To **500 µL total** |

  Calculate the volume of each plasmid from its measured concentration.

**4.** Add the serum-free DMEM to the tube first, followed by all indicated plasmid DNA.

**5.** Add FuGENE HD directly into the media/DNA mixture. Do not pipette the reagent onto the sides of the tube.

**6.** Flick the tube gently to mix.

**7.** Incubate for **10 minutes at room temperature**.

**8.** Add the entire transfection mixture dropwise to the indicated well.

**9.** Incubate cells for approximately **24 hours**, then replace the media with the appropriate selection media.

<details>
  <summary>Next Steps</summary>

  Begin antibiotic selection using the resistance markers encoded by the integrated constructs.

  Expand surviving cells and validate doxycycline-inducible expression of the gene of interest.

</details>

<details>
  <summary>More Info</summary>

  Penicillin/Streptomycin can remain in the cell culture media during FuGENE HD transfection.

  FuGENE HD binds readily to plastic. Dispensing it onto the sides of the tube can reduce transfection efficiency.
